Controls:

Main menu:

Click or tap on the button you want to press

If you have a gamepad, you can push Start to play or Select to explore

Playing mode:

Click and drag or tap and drag anywhere on the game area to move the paddle

If you prefer to use the keyboard, you can play with WASD or the arrow keys

If you have a gamepad, you can play with the left analog stick or the D-pad

Exploration mode:

Click and drag or tap and drag to look around

Use WASD, the arrow keys or the cross on screen to move

If you have a gamepad, you can move around with the left analog stick or the D-pad and look around with the right analog stick

When you are ready to play, click or tap on the button on screen or push Start on your gamepad

Game over:

Click or tap on the button on screen to reset the game

If you have a gamepad, you can push Start to reset
